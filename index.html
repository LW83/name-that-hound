Name That Hound

Welcome to Name that Hound

Name that Hound is a fun quiz to test your knowledge of different dog breeds.


How to Play

  • Enter your name below so we are able to personally congratulate you on your inevitable success
  • Press the Play the Game button below
  • You will be shown an image of a dog and four possible answers for what breed it is
  • Select with answer you think is correct and press the submit button under the options
  • You will be told if your answer is correct and if so a point will be added to your score
  • Once you have answered 10 questions the game is over and you will be taken to your final result
  • If the fun is just too much for you, go right on ahead and hit the play again button to try your luck with another 10 images!
  • If you wish to exit the game at any stage, please click on the icon and it will return you to this Welcome page

We hope you enjoy the game!

Congratulations!
